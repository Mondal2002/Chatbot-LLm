Todung is an enterprise-grade AI Frontdesk platform that automates inbound and outbound customer interactions using human-like conversational AI. It answers calls, books appointments, handles queries, executes workflows, and provides real-time updates to teams, enabling organizations to operate 24/7 without manual frontdesk staffing.

Todung’s vision is to become the default intelligent frontdesk for organizations worldwide. Its mission is to eliminate manual frontdesk inefficiencies through secure, scalable, and industry-ready conversational AI solutions.

Todung provides an always-available AI frontdesk that autonomously answers calls, assists customers, books and manages appointments, automates repetitive interactions, and integrates seamlessly with enterprise systems.

Todung’s conversational AI engine supports natural language understanding with contextual memory, human-like voice modulation, interruption handling, sentiment awareness, and multilingual speech recognition and response.

Todung handles inbound and outbound calls, replaces traditional IVR systems, records and transcribes calls, recovers missed calls automatically, and transfers conversations to human agents when escalation is required.

Todung automates appointment booking, rescheduling, and cancellation. It synchronizes with calendars, sends reminders through calls or messages, and reduces no-shows through automated confirmation workflows.

Todung automates operational workflows such as task creation, CRM updates, ticket generation, and rule-based or AI-driven decision execution without human intervention.

Todung provides real-time alerts to staff via SMS, email, WhatsApp, or applications. Live dashboards display call volume, queues, SLA metrics, and operational load.

Customers initiate interaction by calling or engaging through supported channels. Todung immediately answers and begins conversational interaction without wait times.

Todung identifies callers using available metadata, loads conversation context, detects user intent in real time, and validates required information before proceeding.

Based on detected intent, Todung executes actions such as answering questions, booking appointments, routing calls, triggering workflows, or escalating to human staff.

Todung confirms completed actions with the customer and logs all interactions for analytics, auditing, and compliance purposes.

Todung performs smart triage by understanding patient symptoms and routing them to the appropriate department or medical professional based on urgency and availability.

Todung provides real-time doctor availability, bed status, and department capacity information by integrating with hospital systems.

Todung detects predefined emergency keywords such as severe pain or unconsciousness and triggers priority alerts and instant escalation to medical staff.

Todung automates follow-up calls, medication reminders, appointment confirmations, and patient education using multilingual conversational AI.

Todung greets visitors, verifies appointments, sends staff arrival notifications, and enables touchless check-in for corporate offices.

Todung routes calls to the correct departments, provides employee directory assistance, and automates HR or IT helpdesk queries.

Todung answers banking service queries, provides branch or counter availability information, and supports queue and token-based routing.

Todung assists customers with product information, billing queries, return and exchange policies, and routes calls to service desks.

Todung acts as a digital concierge by assisting guests with check-in guidance, amenities, room services, and multilingual tourist information.

Todung provides department guidance, document requirement explanations, appointment confirmations, and multilingual citizen support for government offices.

Todung assists students and parents with admission queries, course information, office directions, campus navigation, and administrative FAQs.

Todung implements end-to-end encryption, role-based access control, audit logs, data residency options, and enterprise-grade privacy and security practices.

Todung integrates with CRM systems, hospital information systems, ERP platforms, calendars, messaging services, and provides API-based extensibility.

Todung delivers insights on call volume, intent distribution, resolution rates, customer satisfaction indicators, and operational efficiency.
